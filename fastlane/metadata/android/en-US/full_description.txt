<b>BILIBILIAS</b> is an auxiliary tool for Bilibili video caching, providing one-click caching.
Use it to help you cache videos. Currently, we support a considerable number of features to assist you in completing your work.
UP creators can download some clips as material for video editing.
Please note that the project's maintenance may be stopped at any time.
<h3>Supported Features</h3>
<ul>
<li>Scan code login</li>
<li>Cache videos/Bilibili exclusive anime that can be played in Mainland China</li>
<li>Personal Center -&gt; Help cache your own videos</li>
<li>Batch download</li>
<li>Background download</li>
<li>Support ffmpeg</li>
<li>Support download list operations</li>
</ul>
